About this vis

Interaction

Click on one the bars so set the represented airport as your origin airport. The vis will then update to show information using that airport as the origin of your flight.

Color

The color of the bar represents it's Category. The bluer the color, the lower it's category, and the less likely that this flight gets canceled when it departs from that origin airport.

Bar Length

The longer the air port's bar trail, the longer is the flight from the origin aiport to that airport.
