About this View

Interaction

Click on one the bubbles containing airport codes so set that as your origin airport. The vis will then update to show information using that airport as the origin of your flight.

Color

The color of the bubble of the airport represents it's Category. The category is also represented by the ring that it is placed in. The bluer the color, the lower it's category, and the less likely that this flight gets canceled when it departs from that origin airport. Similarly, the outer the ring is in which it's placed, the lower it's priority from that origin airport.
